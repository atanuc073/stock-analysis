## 🔍 Cash Drag Deep-Dive & Regime Mechanics

In quantitative finance, **cash drag** is traditionally viewed as a negative—a drag on compounding because cash earns 0% while equities rise. However, in an active long-only momentum strategy, **dynamic cash drag is your primary risk-management shield**. 

Here is how your engine turned cash drag from a "penalty" into a "weapon":

### 1. The Bear Market Shield (2018 & 2022)
* **2018 (Avg Cash 53.7%):** As the Indian mid-cap universe entered a severe correction, the engine played extremely defensively. It held an average of **53.7% of the book in cash**, clipping up to **85.0%** in cash during peak weakness. This "protective drag" insulated your portfolio from the 30% to 50% grinding losses experienced by broad market participants.
* **2022 (Avg Cash 36.9%):** During the global growth and tech slaughterhouse of 2022, the engine automatically raised cash to an average of **36.9%** (peaking at **78.5%** cash). Volatility was kept at an extremely low **10.9%**, and the year finished with a positive return (+10.9%) while long-only indices suffered major losses.

### 2. The March 2020 COVID Black Swan (90.1% Cash Peak)
* During the vertical liquidity collapse of March 2020, indices fell ~35% in 4 weeks.
* Your engine's regime-aware detectors triggered instantly. The cash percentage skyrocketed to a peak of **90.1%**.
* By liquidating nearly the entire book and shifting to a **90% cash shield**, the engine completely stepped aside during the worst of the panic, keeping your powder dry to buy the generation-defining V-bottom recovery in late 2020!

### 3. Capital Efficiency in Bull Regimes (2021 & 2023-2024)
* **2021 (Avg Cash 10.0%):** In a roaring bull market, cash drag was almost completely eliminated. With an average of 90% of your capital actively deployed in leading momentum names, you captured the full mathematical tailwind (+49.5% return).
* **2023 (Avg Cash 6.3%) & 2024 (Avg Cash 8.2%):** Maximum capital efficiency. The engine deployed nearly 92% to 94% of your total capital into stage-2 breakouts, compounding your ₹50,000 monthly contributions rapidly.
